Access Control & Attendance

Everything Your Office Needs to Track Attendance

We supply, configure, and maintain all attendance and access control hardware ‚Äî from front-door biometrics to full turnstile entry systems.

Fingerprint Attendance Machines

Wall-mounted biometric devices that record employee punch-in/out by fingerprint. Standalone or network-connected, with built-in display and direct HR software export. Brands: ZKTeco, Essl, Realtime.

Face Recognition Terminals

Contactless facial recognition for attendance and access control. Works in variable lighting, supports mask detection, and can read temperatures on select models. No card or fingerprint required.

RFID Card Systems

Proximity and smart card readers for employee access and attendance. Issue ID cards to staff, restrict zone access by department, and view entry logs in real time. Easy to revoke for leavers.

Door Controllers & Electric Locks

Electromagnetic locks, electric strikes, and access control panels for office doors, server rooms, and restricted areas. Supports multi-factor authentication (card + PIN or card + biometric).

Turnstiles & Flap Barriers

Pedestrian access control for reception, lobby, and high-traffic entry points. Tripod turnstiles, full-height turnstiles, and flap barriers with integrated card or biometric readers. Tailgating prevention built in.

Payroll & HR Integration

Connect attendance data directly to payroll software. Auto-calculate working hours, overtime, late arrivals, and half-days. Compatible with popular HRMS platforms and custom Excel exports. No more manual attendance registers.
